Ratios

Ratios explain how quantities compare, scale, and stay balanced. This is the language of proportional thinking.

🎧 Warm-Up

A ratio compares two quantities. It answers the question: how much of one thing exists compared to another?

🧠 The Hack

Ratios are balance statements.

If one side changes, the other must change the same way to stay equivalent.

Example:
2 : 3 → 4 : 6 → 6 : 9
The numbers change, the balance stays.
