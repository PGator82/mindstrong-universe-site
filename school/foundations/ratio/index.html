📌 What This Is & Why It Matters

What this is: Ratios compare two quantities to show how they relate.

Why it matters: Ratios power fractions, rates, percents, and real-world scaling.

🎯 Hook

Two smoothies taste the same. One uses 2 scoops fruit : 3 scoops ice. The other uses 4 scoops fruit : 6 scoops ice.

Different numbers. Same taste. That’s a ratio at work.

🧠 The Hack
