② The Hack

Cross-Multiply to Unlock the Unknown

A proportion looks like this: a/b = c/d. The hack? Cross-multiply — multiply diagonally, set them equal, solve.

⚡ The Hack
If a/b = c/d, then a × d = b × c
Multiply the diagonals. They're always equal.
3/2 = ?/8
Step 1: Cross-multiply → 3 × 8 = 2 × ?
Step 2: Simplify → 24 = 2 × ?
Step 3: Divide → ? = 12

✅ You need 12 cups of flour for 8 people.

The cross-multiply trick works because both fractions represent the same rate — so their cross-products must be equal.
