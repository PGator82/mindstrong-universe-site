② The Hack

Undo the Operations — Work Backwards

An equation is built up using operations. To solve it, undo them in reverse order. Always do the same thing to both sides.

⚡ The Hack — Inverse Operations
Addition ↔ Subtraction (they cancel each other)
Multiplication ↔ Division (they cancel each other)

Rule: Whatever you do to one side, do to the other.
Goal: Get x alone on one side.
1
Start: 3x + 5 = 20
2
Subtract 5 from both sides: 3x = 15
3
Divide both sides by 3: x = 5
✓
Check: 3(5) + 5 = 15 + 5 = 20 ✅
Always check your answer by substituting back in.
If both sides equal the same number, you're done. If not, find the error.
