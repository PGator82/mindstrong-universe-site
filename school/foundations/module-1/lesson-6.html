Estimation as a Superpower

Smell test • Reasonableness

Mode:

Estimation isn't giving up on precision — it's a sanity check. Before you calculate, ask: what's the ballpark? If your answer is wildly off, something went wrong.

📚 Core Concepts

👃
Smell Test
Before computing 497 × 3, estimate: 500 × 3 = 1,500. Your real answer should be close to this.
⚡
Front-End Estimation
Use the leading digits. 3,847 + 2,156 ≈ 4,000 + 2,000 = 6,000.
🤔
Reasonableness Check
If your answer is way off from the estimate, there's a mistake somewhere.
