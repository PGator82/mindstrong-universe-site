Place Value Is a Volume Knob

×10 louder • ÷10 quieter

Mode:

Our number system is like a volume knob — every place is exactly 10× louder than the one to its right. The digit 4 in 4,000 is playing at a completely different volume than 4 in 40.

📚 Core Concepts

🔊
Place Value
Ones → Tens → Hundreds → Thousands. Each step left is ×10.
📊
Digit vs Value
The digit 5 is always 5. But its VALUE depends on where it sits: 5 vs 500 vs 5,000.
📍
Decimals Too
Tenths → Hundredths → Thousandths. Each step right is ÷10.
