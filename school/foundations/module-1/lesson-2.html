Lesson 2 — Place Value Is a Volume Knob

Each place to the left makes the number louder. To the right, quieter.

Next Lesson → Back to Foundations
Status: Not completed

Hook

Adding a zero doesn’t change digits — it changes power.

5 → 50 → 500 → 5,000

The Hack

Moving left = ×10 (louder)
Moving right = ÷10 (quieter)

🎮 Play Mode — Volume Knob

Turn the volume knob and watch the number scale.

5
Base number: 5
Multiplier: ×1

Each step = one place value shift.

Practice

  1. What happens when you move right twice?
  2. Why is 500 ten times louder than 50?
  3. Explain why 0.5 is quieter than 5.

Exit Check

Describe place value without saying “zero”.
