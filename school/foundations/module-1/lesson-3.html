Lesson 3 — Expanded Form

Big numbers are built from smaller pieces.

Hook

The number 432 looks like one thing — but it’s actually three numbers working together.

Expanded form lets us see the hidden structure.

The Hack

Every digit has a job.

Expanded form = digit × its place value.

🎮 Play Mode — Number LEGO

Type a number. Watch it break into place-value blocks.

Practice

  1. Write 705 in expanded form.
  2. Which digit contributes the most in 3,482?
  3. Why does the 0 still matter in 508?

Exit Check

Explain expanded form using the phrase “place-value blocks.”
