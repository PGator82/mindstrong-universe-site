Lesson 1 — Numbers Are Stories

Numbers aren’t just symbols. They’re meaning + size.

Next Lesson → Back to Foundations
Status: Not completed

Hook

Numbers tell stories about size, distance, and amount.

9 vs 900 — same digits, different story.

The Hack

Every number answers: “How much compared to something familiar?”

  • 10 ≈ hands
  • 100 ≈ steps
  • 1,000 ≈ minutes in a week

🎮 Play Mode — Magnitude Game

Move the slider. Decide if the number is small, medium, or large.

Number: 100
Magnitude: Medium

Rules:
• 1–99 = Small
• 100–999 = Medium
• 1000+ = Large

Practice

  1. Which feels bigger: 450 or 4,500?
  2. Which is closer to 1,000: 875 or 1,200?
  3. Write a story for 3,000.

Exit Check

Explain the difference between digits and magnitude.
