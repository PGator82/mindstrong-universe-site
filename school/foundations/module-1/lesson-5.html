Lesson 5 — Rounding That Makes Sense

📌 What This Is & Why It Matters

What this is: Learning how to round numbers using landmarks.

Why it matters: Helps estimate quickly and avoid bad answers.

Hook

Is 478 closer to 400 or 500?

The Hack

Find the nearest landmark (0, 100, 1,000).

🎮 Play Mode — Rounding Radar

Practice

  1. Round 842 to the nearest hundred.
  2. Round 1,499 to the nearest thousand.

Exit Check

Explain rounding without saying “five or more”.
