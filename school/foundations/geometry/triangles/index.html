Triangles

Three sides. Three angles. One rule that never breaks: they always add to 180°.

① Hook

The Strongest Shape in Engineering

Bridges. Rooftops. Guitar bodies. Crash zones in cars. They all use triangles. Why? Because a triangle cannot be deformed without changing the length of a side. It's the only shape that's inherently rigid.

But every triangle — no matter how it's drawn, stretched, or rotated — has one thing in common. Its three angles always add to exactly 180°. Always. This is the law we exploit.
