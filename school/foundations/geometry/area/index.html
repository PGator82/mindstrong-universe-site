Fencing a Yard vs Painting a Floor

You want to fence a rectangular yard that is 12m × 8m. You also want to tile the floor. Two different jobs — two different calculations.

Fencing = perimeter (go around the edge). Tiling = area (fill the surface). Mixing these up wastes money and materials.

⚡ The Key Insight
Perimeter = add ALL sides
Area = 2D space inside the shape
Triangle area = ½ × base × height (half a rectangle!)
